STAT 140: Introduction to the Ideas and Applications of Statistics

Welcome to STAT 140! In this course, you will be introduced to Statistics, a scientific discipline that focuses on the collection and analysis of data to draw conclusions about populations of interest. Some statisticians describe how they think about Statistics here. Statistics can be used to answer important questions through careful data collection and analysis in nearly every discipline, including agriculture, bench sciences, business, law, psychology, and public health. They also routinely pop up in the news. As such, it is important to have the skills to think critically about what data can and cannot tell us, and to be an informed consumer and producer of quantitative/statistical analyses.
