About

I am an Assistant Professor of Statistics in the Department of Mathematics & Statistics at Mount Holyoke College. I completed my Ph.D. in Biostatistics at the University of Iowa, College of Public Health in 2019, under the supervision of Grant Brown and Jacob Oleson. I specialize in Bayesian infectious disease modeling and collaborate with the Petersen Laboratory and the Department of Biostatistics at the University of Iowa. My other research interests include spatial statistics and longitudinal data.
